# Finance-Specific Detectors

## Phase segmentation
Opening → Discovery → Positioning → Objections → Close/Next Step.

## Finance discovery ladder (prompt coach to look for)
1) Cashflow & Safety Net: income, fixed vs variable expenses, emergency fund months.
2) Protection: death/TPD/CI coverage, medical, dependents.
3) Investing: horizon, risk tolerance, diversification, liquidity needs.
4) Goals: retirement age/income, education funding, housing, legacy.
5) Constraints: fees sensitivity, past product experiences, compliance limits.
6) Quantify: monthly surplus, target corpus, gap math (rough, non-advisory).

## Evidence cues to quote
- Numbers: "$X/month", "% contribution", "Y years", "Z coverage".
- Risk/fee language: "too expensive", "ETF cheaper", "lock-in", "surrender".
- Past experiences: "burned before", "agent disappeared", "policy lapsed".
- Time markers: "before 60", "next 12 months", "after BMT".

## Objection patterns (with coach prompts)
- Fees/Value: "High fees" → contrast total cost vs. service scope; show when fees buy risk mgmt/behavioral coaching/admin.
- Liquidity/Lock-in: "Can't access funds" → match to goal horizon; propose split buckets.
- DIY vs Product: "I can just buy ETFs" → fair contrast: effort, risk discipline, insurance gaps, rebalancing, behavior risk.
- Performance Guarantees: "Can you guarantee X%" → compliance language; reframe to risk bands and goals.
- Insurance Charges/ILP: "Charges too high" → clarify structure, what charges fund (admin/risk), alternatives (term + invest), suitability.
- Bad Past Experience: "Agent ghosted me" → acknowledge, show service cadence & transparent docs.
