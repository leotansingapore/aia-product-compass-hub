# CPF Video Course → Next 60 Days Mapping

The Basic CPF Knowledge video course (19 videos, `/product/basic-cpf-knowledge` on the hub) is the **canonical CPF mastery deep-dive**. The Next 60 Days track is for generalist post-RNF FCs — not pre-retiree specialists — but several days benefit from concrete CPF-flavored *examples* drawn from those videos.

This file is the index. Specialist CPF coverage lives in:

- `Obsidian Vault/Content/pre-retiree-sales-track/` — the dedicated 30-day pre-retiree track (where the videos drove curriculum-level enhancements, see [MAPPING-30D.md](../../../../Obsidian%20Vault/Content/pre-retiree-sales-track/_source-supplementary/basic-cpf-knowledge-video-course/MAPPING-30D.md))
- `docs/first-60-days/_source-supplementary/basic-cpf-knowledge-video-course/` — local transcripts (gitignored, see READMEs for index)

## Why these four days specifically

Most Next 60 Days lessons are angle-agnostic and don't benefit from a pre-retiree-specific example. The four selected days have the strongest *natural hook* — the existing content already references retirement, the 55-year-old client, or restructuring in a context where CPF is the load-bearing variable. Adding a concrete CPF example at those points makes the principle land harder without bloating the lesson.

## Where to read the source material

- Vault: `Obsidian Vault/Content/pre-retiree-sales-track/_source-supplementary/basic-cpf-knowledge-video-course/` (committed)

## What's *not* in this mapping

- **Days 7-12 (Intent Statement, Activity Math, Story, Tonality)** — pre-retiree-segment-agnostic. Mentioning CPF would be forced.
- **Days 13-21 (Prospecting / Outreach)** — same; the pre-retiree angle is one of 5 personas, not a focus.
- **Days 28-36 (Scripts, Flywheel, Hot Buttons I)** — generic principles; CPF examples don't add power over what's already there.
- **Days 56-60 (Endgame / Self-mastery)** — process-level, not segment-level.

If a generalist FC works mainly with pre-retirees, they should escalate to the dedicated 30-day pre-retiree-sales-track in the Obsidian vault rather than try to extract specialist depth from the Next 60 Days curriculum.
