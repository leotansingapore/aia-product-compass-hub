*Stop selling the way you buy. 5-minute speed-read + profile-tailored opener + profile-matched close — all in one week.*

## Unlock rule for Week 7

Week 7 unlocks when you (1) record 4 separate 90-second roleplays, one per profile, each showing the tailored opener AND matched close, (2) submit a written self-assessment naming your primary/secondary profile + 2 counter-programming gaps, (3) record the Day-36 Loom reflection, and (4) book a 15-min mentor tape review.

The mentor review is longer than usual because this is the first week they watch footage with you.

---

## Sunday 30-minute review

Three prompts:

1. **Which roleplay scored lowest on *pace/energy match*?** That's the counter-program you train in Week 7 live.
2. **Which profile do you *most often miss* in real meetings?** Usually your opposite profile on both axes (D ↔ S, or I ↔ C).
3. **Pick 3 warm-market names** for Week 7 practice — one of each profile that's NOT your own. These become the testbeds for the hot-button work.

## What's new in the consolidated Week 6

Previously, DISC ran across Weeks 6 and 7 (overview + 4 profiles, then speed-read + tailoring). The speed-read rule (5-min window + 3-signals-commit) now lives in Day 31, and each profile day (32–35) now includes the *tailored opener* + *matched close technique* for that profile. Same content, one week, integrated by profile.

---

## What's coming in Week 7

**Hot Buttons + Pitch Mechanics.** Week 6 taught profile recognition + matched openers/closes. Week 7 pairs the profile read with the *hot button* (what emotionally drives the buying decision), trains the full objection-turnaround toolkit (Anchor-Disrupt-Ask + ART + Iceberg), and ties it together with confidence language and the 4 closes. By Saturday you'll have delivered 3 full pitches to 3 different profiles with 3 different hot buttons.

**Week 7 KPI:** 3 pitches delivered — 3 different DISC types, 3 different hot buttons.
