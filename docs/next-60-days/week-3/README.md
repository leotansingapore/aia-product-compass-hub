## Unlock rule for Week 4

Week 4 unlocks when you (1) ship 3 IG posts answering different silent questions (Q5 offer post required), (2) open 5 DM conversations with real exchanges — mentor / peer conversations don't count, (3) submit the Day-18 screenshot log + Loom reflection, and (4) book a 10-minute mentor review.

## Sunday 30-minute review

Three prompts to close the week:

1. **Which of the 5 silent questions is your content weakest on?** Look at the week's 3 posts plus the last 10 you already had. The gap question is Week 4's first draft priority.
2. **Which DM opener type landed best for your warm market?** Hook-based, value-first, offer-post reply, or CRAB? That's the one you lean on in Week 4.
3. **Which 5 warm-market names are next?** Week 4 doubles the pace — 30 outreaches, 5 appointments booked. You'll need a longer list ready.
