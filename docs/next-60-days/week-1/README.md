## Sunday 30-minute review (rest day, but not off-the-grid)

Sunday is a rest day. That said, give yourself 30 minutes to close the week. Three prompts:

1. **What did you commit to this week that actually happened?** Be honest. The gap between intention and execution is the data.
2. **What's on the scorecard this week?** Calls / Appointments / Referrals — put the numbers next to each. These are your baseline for Week 2's KPI of *intent statement v2 delivered to 3 real people.*
3. **Who are 3 people from your warm market who'd be easiest to approach with the Day-6 intro?** Write the names. You'll reach out in Week 2.
