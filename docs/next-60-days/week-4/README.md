## Unlock rule for Week 5

Week 5 unlocks when you (1) complete 30 substantive outreaches across a mix of approaches (MS calls, 6-step messages, Attraction, CRAB, value drops — no padding), (2) book 5 Fact-Find appointments with confirmed time + format + both-sides acknowledgment, (3) submit the Friday pipeline review screenshot (CAR numbers + target list status), (4) record the Loom reflection, and (5) book a 10-minute mentor review.

Soft bookings and padded outreach don't count.

---

## Sunday 30-minute review

Three prompts to close the week:

1. **Which approach converted best?** Market Survey, 6-step message, Attraction, CRAB, or value drops? That's what you lean on in Week 5 — while keeping 2–3 secondary channels active.
2. **Which Market Temperature ring is most alive for you?** If most bookings came from Warm, your Semi-Warm reactivation is Week 5's next frontier. If Semi-Warm is working, lean harder — it's the 66% ring.
3. **Is the Strategic Target List refreshed for Week 5?** 20 new names + trigger + approach ready by Sunday night. Monday morning should never be *"who do I call?"*

## What's coming in Week 5

**Referrals From Day One.** Week 4 built the outreach engine. Week 5 builds the *compounding* engine — asking for referrals at every Fact-Find so Week 6's pipeline isn't entirely outbound effort. The FACT method, coaching clients what to say, and the CAR flywheel are the core.

**Week 5 KPI:** 10 referral asks made; 3 referrals received.
