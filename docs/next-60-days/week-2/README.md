## Unlock rule for Week 3

Week 3 unlocks when you (1) deliver your intent statement to 3 real prospects — not mentors, not peers — (2) log each delivery (3 lines × 3 = 9-line log), (3) submit the Day-12 Loom reflection, and (4) book a 10-minute mentor review.

No deliveries = no unlock. Roleplay does not count.

---

## Sunday 30-minute review

Three prompts to close the week:

1. **Did all 3 deliveries happen?** If not, which one didn't — and why? The blocker you hit this week will show up again in Week 3; name it now.
2. **Which ingredient of your intent statement needs the most work?** Hook, impression, pattern interrupt, or buy-in. Pick one. That's what v3 will fix.
3. **Which 3 people from your warm market are next?** Week 3 takes the same statement into the DM funnel and digital-trust work — you'll need another 3 names ready.
