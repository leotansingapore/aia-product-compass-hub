# Week 6 — Financial Fundamentals

**The week's thesis:** Every client conversation you'll have for the rest of your career sits on top of this week's math. Present Value, Rule of 72, CPF, investment types, inflation — these aren't separate topics. They're the integrated foundation that turns you from a salesperson into an advisor.

- [ ] Logged into your own CPF statement; know your balances

## The single line to remember from Week 6

> "The risk isn't market volatility. The risk is not keeping up with inflation."
