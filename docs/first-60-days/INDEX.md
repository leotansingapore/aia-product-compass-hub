**Total:** 60 days, ~87,000 words of training content.

## How to Use This Module

### Daily cadence
- **Monday–Saturday:** one day of content per day.
- **Sunday:** rest + 30-min weekly review.
- **Target time per day:** 20 minutes of reading + 15–30 min on the worksheet.

### Each day contains
1. **One big idea** (the pull-quote at top).
2. **3 learning objectives.**
3. **Digestible content** (tables, frameworks, worked examples).
4. **Reflection worksheet** — don't skip.
5. **3-question quiz** with answers.

### Minimum to do it well
- Actually write the worksheet answers.
- Practise the scripts aloud.
- Apply the day's content in a real situation within 72 hours.
- Review weekly with your mentor.

### What "graduated" means
- You've completed all 60 days.
- You've built the weekly rhythms.
- You're running live activity (Project 100 calls, first meetings, first proposals).
- You have first-hand reps on the full arc: Approach → SPIN → Recommendation → Close.

## After the 60 Days

Day 60 is not the end of training. It's the **beginning of the career.** The next 60 days are where you apply what you learned, at volume, with reps.

Your Year 1 target: $50K+ FYC, 20+ closed clients, referral flywheel started.

Your Year 3 target: MDRT, 150+ active clients, renewal income meaningful.

Your Year 5 target: a sustainable book, established reputation, a real second income stream from recurring commissions.

**The career is now yours to build.**
