# Your First 14 Days

*By the end of 14 days, you'll know — with real conviction — whether this career is for you, and whether our team is the right home for it.*

Hey. I'm Leo.

I've been in your shoes. I was 21, fresh out of NS, staring at a fork in the road. Perfect A-levels, scholarship on the table, NUS Engineering ahead of me — and a nagging question: *why should I wait 8 to 10 years to earn $10K a month when I could start building that income right now?*

I took the plunge. Year 1 of university, I earned **$145K** as a full-time engineering student working this part-time. By graduation, I'd crossed $1M. Today I've served 1,002 clients, hit MDRT 5 times, COT 3 times, and built four other companies on top of the financial-advisory business — including Singapore's #1 ranked marketing agency.

I'm not telling you this to impress you. I'm telling you this so you know the person writing these 14 days has actually done it — and is not running a theoretical pitch.

This course exists because **I wish I had it when I was 21.** I had to figure it out alone — no scripts, no systems, no mentor. That took me 3–4 years longer than it needed to. My goal with this 14-day read is to cut your learning curve by those same 3–4 years, and let you decide — with your eyes fully open — whether this is for you.

## What this is

- **14 days, ~15 minutes each.** Weekdays, weekends, your pace.
- **No signup, no form, no one is calling you.** This is just reading.
- **One worksheet per day, for you only.** Nothing submitted.
- **A quiz at the end of each day** so the ideas actually stick.
- **By Day 14, a clear decision:** Commit, Delay, Keep Exploring, or honest No.

## What happens at the end

By Day 14 you'll be in one of four honest places:

1. **Commit.** You're ready. Next step: a real conversation with me. Book an onboarding call.
2. **Delay.** The career fits, the timing doesn't. You'll have a specific date to revisit.
3. **Keep exploring.** You like the model, not sure about the agency. Day 8 gives you the criteria.
4. **Honest No.** This isn't for you. That's a legitimate outcome — you've saved yourself 12 months.

Only three of those lead to joining us. The fourth is still a win — it gave you clarity. That's the whole point of doing this properly.
