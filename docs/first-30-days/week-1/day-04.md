## Reflection worksheet

**1. What are the top 3 goals on your vision board? Why do these matter to you personally?**
> Your vision board should reflect what truly motivates you — not just financial targets but lifestyle and personal growth goals.

**2. In your business plan, what is your target for the first 3 months? Is it realistic given your current network?**
> Be honest about your starting position and adjust your plan accordingly.
