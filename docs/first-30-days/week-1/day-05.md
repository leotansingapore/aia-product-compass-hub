## Project 100 & Warm Prospecting

[loom video](https://www.loom.com/share/679b8b0aba404b0d80e8e446314ac51c)

### Step 1: Build Your Warm Database

Download and duplicate the [Warm Database Sheet](https://docs.google.com/spreadsheets/d/1Bm0WQMPWggZ7e4o_MO-yfLxJCVvVgHd1/edit?usp=sharing&ouid=117605838416133603235&rtpof=true&sd=true) and start thinking about how to reach out to your contacts — for a catch-up, meal, etc. — with the objective of seeing what existing policies and gaps they have.

> **Key insight:** Instead of focusing on your "hot" market, focus on your **warm/semi-warm** contacts. Most of your business will come from semi-warm leads — people you haven't spoken to in years. These are the most untapped form of market.

![Warm vs Hot Market](https://assets.skool.com/f/8fc2f6045ea549398401f3cdbcc90878/214dd29704784a1bb966fc3961ac1236fb769da345274a4bab84c4464fc21d7f-md.png)

### Step 3: Build Your Pipeline

The idea is to build up enough cases so that you can **start closing the moment your contract/EPS starts**.

> **Action required:** Continually update your onboarding group chat with upcoming cases — friends you're meeting — so the team can support on how to reach out or structure a portfolio for them.
